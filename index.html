Waste management is a critical issue facing communities worldwide. With increasing population growth and consumption patterns, the volume of waste produced has reached unsustainable levels, threatening our environment and public health. Proper waste management involves the collection, transportation, disposal, and recycling of waste in ways that minimize harm to the environment. Effective waste management systems are essential to reducing pollution, conserving natural resources, and protecting ecosystems.
